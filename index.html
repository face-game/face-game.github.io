The Face Game

Just move your head around and do what is says to do, ok?

Having trouble?
  • Try moving your face closer or farther from the camera.
  • Look straight in the direction of the camera.
  • Make sure that your beautiful face is well-lit and that there is nothing behind you that is brighter than your face (ex. lights, windows, screens, etc.).
